# Plan: Research and Masterplan

## Assignment
Research the forest bathing concept, competitive landscape, target markets, and the operator's properties. Write masterplan and full assignment queue.

## Research summary

### Concept
Shinrin-yoku coined 1982 by Japanese Forestry Agency. Scientific evidence is real but measured:
- Proven: cortisol reduction, lower blood pressure/heart rate, improved mood/anxiety (most replicated findings)
- Solid but nuanced: autonomic nervous system effects, NK cell activity (Li's research)
- Not proven: long-term disease prevention, specific phytoncide mechanism in humans
Best framing: evidence-based wellness, not therapy. Cite specific measurable effects, honest about what's preliminary.

Key terminology:
- English: "forest bathing" (most mainstream), "shinrin-yoku" (signals authenticity)
- German: "Waldbaden" (dominant), "Shinrin-yoku" used alongside
- Swedish: "skogsbad" (primary), "naturterapi" (clinical)
- Dutch: "bosbaden" / Danish: "skovbadning"

### Competitive landscape
- No authoritative editorial hub for forest bathing travel in Scandinavia — market wide open
- Existing sites are either: (a) US/UK guide certification orgs, (b) single-venue retreats, (c) generic booking platforms
- No site owns "forest bathing Sweden", "Waldbaden Schweden", or "skogsbad Sverige" — all low competition
- Canopy & Stars (canopyandstars.co.uk) is the structural model: editorial voice + curated listings

### Target markets (priority order)
1. German (largest, strongest Waldbaden cultural fit, major Sweden travellers)
2. Dutch (second largest, very similar to German, possibly easier ROI per euro spent)
3. Swedish domestic (essential baseline)
4. Danish (proximity-driven, short breaks)
5. English (international catchall)

### Low-budget marketing channels
- SEO: own the long-tail keywords (nearly zero competition for Blekinge-specific terms)
- German Facebook groups: "Waldbaden Deutschland", "Schweden Urlaub & Reisen" (genuine engagement)
- Visit Blekinge: regional tourism org, co-marketing opportunities, press trip programs
- Visit Sweden: German market program, press trip program
- German Scandinavia travel bloggers (micro-influencers in nature/wellness space)

### Properties
**Kringelgården** — Historic farmhouse c.1790, renovated. 3 bedrooms, 7 beds, 1 bath, up to 8 guests. Lake Bredasjön 200m away, rowing boat. Ground floor: dining room with fireplace, library with lake view, kitchen with wood-fired stove. Upper floor: themed hallway, master bedroom with lake view, children's bedroom, additional bedroom.

**Drängstugan** — Secluded forest cottage. 2 bedrooms, 4 beds, 1 bath, up to 6 guests. Same forest and lake setting. Main room: kitchen/dining/sofa/fireplace. Rating: 5.0/5 (4 reviews). Pets allowed. Review quotes: "forest and fields and just peace", "tranquility perfect and unique".

Combined: up to 14 guests (airbnb.com/h/kringelgarden-all)

Key copy from operator's Airbnb: "The forest surrounding the farm has been cared for by my family for more than 100 years. I focus on recreation and nature experiences rather than timber production, and my father did the same in his time. The result is a varied, calm, and beautiful forest landscape, perfect for walks, swimming, berry picking, and simply being in nature."

### Brand name analysis
"Waldenbad" = Walden (Thoreau) + bad (bath in Swedish/German = forest bath = Shinrin-yoku). Works across all 5 languages.

## Strategy
Position waldenbad.se as the authoritative editorial home for forest bathing travel in Sweden. Genuine editorial content + curated accommodation listings. Kringelgården and Drängstugan are the commercial anchor. Tone: down-to-earth, evidence-based, no mysticism.
